Hermes Agent is your personal AI on iOS. Instead of juggling apps, tabs, and copy-paste, you describe what needs to happen in plain language and the agent takes care of the rest — drafting and sending emails, booking calendar events, running web research, and dozens of other actions across your connected accounts. Everything happens in real time, with your approval before anything irreversible goes out.

## Get up and running

New to Hermes Agent? Follow these four steps and you'll have your first task running in minutes.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Download the app">
    Search for **Hermes Agent** on the App Store and install it on your iPhone. The app requires iOS 16 or later.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Sign in with Apple & redeem your invite">
    Open the app and tap **Sign in with Apple**. After authenticating, enter your invite code when prompted. The app will provision your personal AI agent — this takes less than a minute.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect an account">
    Tap the gear icon in the top-right corner, go to **Connections**, and connect a service such as Gmail. Complete the OAuth flow and you're ready.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create your first task">
    Tap the **+** button, type what you need done in plain language, and tap **Save**. Open the task and tap **Do it** to watch your agent get to work.
  </Step>
</Steps>
