---

This guide walks you through everything you need to go from a fresh install to a completed, AI-executed task. By the end, you'll have the app installed, your account connected, and a real task running — all in under five minutes.

<Info>
  **Before you begin, you'll need:**
  - An iPhone running **iOS 16 or later**
  - An **Apple ID** signed in on the device
  - A valid **Hermes Agent invite code** — if you don't have one yet, join the waitlist at [hermesagent.app](https://hermesagent.app)
</Info>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Download the app">
    Open the **App Store** on your iPhone and search for **Hermes Agent**. Tap **Get** to install it.

    Hermes Agent is invite-only during early access, so you won't be able to create an account without a valid invite code — but the app itself is free to download.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Sign in with Apple">
    Open Hermes Agent and tap **Sign in with Apple** on the welcome screen. Confirm your identity with **Face ID** or **Touch ID** when prompted.

    Hermes Agent never sees your Apple password. Sign in with Apple gives you a private, secure login with no new credentials to remember.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Redeem your invite code">
    After signing in, the app will ask for your invite code. Type or paste it in and tap **Redeem**.

    The app will provision your personal AI agent in the background — this takes less than a minute. You'll see a confirmation screen when your agent is ready.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Allow notifications">
    Tap **Allow** when the system notification prompt appears. Notifications let your agent tell you when a task finishes, even if you've closed the app.

    You can adjust notification preferences at any time in **Settings > Notifications** on your iPhone.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Connect an account">
    Tap the **gear icon** in the top-right corner to open Settings, then tap **Connections**. Tap **Connect** next to **Gmail** (or any other service you'd like to start with) and complete the OAuth authorization flow in the browser sheet that appears.

    Once the flow completes, you'll see the connection marked as **Active**. Your agent can now send email on your behalf — with your approval.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Create your first task">
    Tap the **+** button at the bottom of the task list. In the text field, type a plain-language instruction, for example:

    > "Email my landlord that rent will be 3 days late — be polite."

    Tap **Save**. The task appears in your list, ready to run.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Watch it work">
    Tap the task to open it, then tap **Do it**. The **Activity** section below the task detail will begin filling with live steps as your agent works — searching contacts, drafting the email, and preparing to send.

    You don't need to keep the app open. Your agent keeps working in the background and will notify you when it needs your input or when it's done.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Approve the action">
    When the agent has drafted the email, it will pause and show you an **approval card** with the full draft. Review the content, then tap **Send** to approve and deliver it — or tap **Rewrite** to give the agent feedback and regenerate, or **Cancel** to discard.

    The email is sent only after you approve. Your agent never takes irreversible action without your explicit sign-off.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  You don't have to connect an account before creating a task. If the agent needs a connection that isn't set up yet, it will pause mid-task and prompt you to authenticate inline — no need to visit Settings first.
</Tip>

Now that you've completed your first task, explore the [Task Detail guide](/using-the-app/task-detail) to learn more about the approval flow, how to rewrite agent output, and how to set tasks to run on a schedule.
