Hermes Agent is a personal AI assistant that lives on your iPhone and acts on your behalf across the apps and services you already use. Unlike a chatbot that simply answers questions, Hermes Agent takes real action — it reads your context, decides which tools to use, calls those tools with your connected accounts, and reports back with results. You stay in control at every step: the agent always asks for your approval before sending anything or making a change you can't undo.

## How it works

Getting something done with Hermes Agent is as simple as describing it. You type (or speak) what you need — the agent handles everything from there.

1. **You describe the task.** Type a plain-language instruction like "Email my landlord that rent will be 3 days late — be polite." You can also use voice input or attach a photo for context.
2. **The agent prepares.** It rewrites the title into a clear action, identifies which integration it needs, and may ask a short clarifying question if something is ambiguous.
3. **The agent executes.** It calls the right tools — Gmail, Calendar, web search, and so on — using your connected accounts, and streams each step live in the Activity feed so you can follow along.
4. **You approve irreversible actions.** Before sending an email, creating an event, or posting a message, the agent pauses and shows you a preview. You can approve, edit, or cancel.
5. **You're notified when it's done.** A push notification and a Live Activity on your Lock Screen let you know the task is complete, even if you've switched to another app.

## Operating modes

Hermes Agent supports three operating modes to fit different privacy, infrastructure, and control needs.

| Mode | Description | Best for |
|---|---|---|
| **Hosted** | Fully managed by the Hermes team — no setup required. Batteries included. | Most users who want a zero-config experience. |
| **BYO Connector** | You run your own Hermes gateway (connector) and point the app at it. The AI and account credentials flow through your infrastructure. | Power users and small teams who want data to stay on their own servers. |
| **Self-Host** | Full control — you host both the connector and the AI model backend. Nothing leaves your environment unless you want it to. | Enterprises and privacy-first users with DevOps capability. |
